title: "Self Hosting"
description: "Deploy StateBase in your VPC"
---

StateBase is available as a Docker container for Enterprise customers.

## Docker Compose

```yaml
version: '3.8'
services:
  statebase:
    image: ghcr.io/statebase/core:latest
    environment:
      - PG_URI=postgresql://...
      - REDIS_URI=redis://...
    ports:
      - "8000:8000"
```

Contact sales@statebase.org for a license key.
